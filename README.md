# Amazon Sales Analysis Project

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Amazon sales data using SQL to solve real-world business problems. The analysis focuses on customer spending behavior, revenue trends, product performance, customer ranking, and repeat purchase patterns.

The project demonstrates practical SQL skills used in Data Analyst and Data Scientist roles, including:

* Aggregations
* Window Functions
* CASE Statements
* GROUP BY & HAVING
* Ranking Functions
* Revenue Analysis
* Customer Segmentation
* Business Insights

---

# Business Questions Solved

### Q1. What was the actual revenue of each order after discount?

Calculated the final revenue generated from every order after applying discounts.

### Q2. What is the total spending of each customer? (Prime vs Regular)

Compared customer spending based on membership type to identify high-value customers.

### Q3. What is the category-wise revenue and percentage contribution to total revenue?

Analyzed which product categories contribute the most to overall business revenue.

### Q4. What is the month-wise revenue trend from January to April?

Tracked monthly revenue performance to identify growth trends and seasonal patterns.

### Q5. What is the customer ranking overall and within membership tiers?

Used ranking functions to rank customers based on their total spending.

### Q6. Which customers are repeat customers with 2 or more orders?

Identified loyal customers who placed multiple orders.

### Q7. What is the best-selling product in each category?

Found the top-performing product for every category based on sales.
